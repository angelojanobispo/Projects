Financial Report file - the actual Power BI dashboard where you can open and edit.




This dashboard solves the following problem:

1. What is our total revenue performance for the reporting period?
2. How many total units have we sold across all markets and segments?
3. Are we achieving overall profitability, and what is our current profit/loss position?
4. What revenue trends are emerging month over month, and are there any significant spikes or declines that require attention?
5. Which business segments are driving the highest revenue contribution to the organization?
6. How are discount bands impacting overall unit sales performance?
7. Which countries are contributing the strongest results in terms of revenue, units sold, and profit/loss?
8 Which business segments are delivering the best financial performance based on revenue, units sold, and profit/loss?
